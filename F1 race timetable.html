Formula 1 Grand Prix Timetable for 2018

Formula 1 in 2018 is scheduled to have 21 races.

With the departure of The Sepang circuit in Malaysia, the race returns to Circuit Paul Ricard in France. Along with the return of The French GP which was last held at this circuit in 1990, the Hockenheimring at Germany also makes it return to the race calendar in 2018 after a year's absence.
